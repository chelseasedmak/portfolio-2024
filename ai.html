AI Panel
User Experience Design
User Research

An AI that offers personalized investment tips and real-time risk management, helping users feel confident in their financial choices.

about
While collaborating with a UX researcher, we discovered that certain information on the company's invoicing page is hard to locate, which slows users down and makes it challenging to complete tasks efficiently. For instance, finding a customer's shipping address or identifying the sales representative for a specific order can be very time-consuming due to limited search options. The goal of this project is to develop an artificial intelligence interface that provides information instantly, enabling users to complete tasks and respond to customers more quickly. This will boost productivity and reduce frustration for users.
01
useflow + wireframes
I created a user flow and wireframes to ensure we understood each step of the process. There was a lot of iteration during this phase, and trust me, iteration is your friend! It’s especially important to go through this process when developing user flows and wireframes, as it helps uncover problems or challenges that might not be obvious at first. By embracing this iterative approach, you can save time and resources in the long run.
02
components
The components in the company’s existing design system were created using an open-source frontend framework. For this project, I applied atomic design principles. This means starting with small components and then building larger ones from those. Once the components are finalized, they’re arranged on templates, which are then used to create the final pages.
03
prototype
While users can ask any questions related to their company's data, the panel’s standout features include: 1) a collapsible sidebar for a broader view of information, 2) chat history for quick access to previously asked questions, 3) the ability to export chats and conversations directly to the user’s email, and 4) a chat edit button to easily generate new information.
