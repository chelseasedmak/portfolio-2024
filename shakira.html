about
A simple, minimalist website to promote Shakira's GRAMMY-nominated album, "Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran." The design focuses on a clean, modern aesthethic and easy navigation, showcasing the music and highlighting her artistry throughout the yeara.
01
branding
The client wanted a clean and simple website to showcase her latest album, "Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran," which features various versions named after gemstones, particularly diamonds. To maintain a minimalist look while adding some flair, I incorporated elements that reflect the diamond theme, enhancing the design without overwhelming it. This approach kept the site user-friendly and inviting, capturing the vibe of her newest album.
02
website design
Each page of the design is easy to navigate and straightforward. The goal was to ensure that users can quickly find her music, videos, and tour information without any hassle. This streamlined approach keeps the focus on what matters most: connecting fans with Shakira's art and upcoming events.
03
details
The details are kept minimal, but I incorporated small animations that enhance the site without disrupting its clean vibe or user experience. These subtle touches add to the overall feel of the album, creating a dynamic yet balanced environment that reflects Shakira's artistic vision.
