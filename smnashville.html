A easy-to-navigate website for a major record label that helps visitors easily find the latest music releases and news.

about
A website built for Sony Music Nashville, designed to be clean and user-friendly. The client wanted a layout that allows visitors to quickly find what they need while also showcasing the latest information, music releases, and news. This straightforward approach ensures that users can easily navigate the site and stay updated on all things related to their favorite artists.
01
homepage
The homepage is simple yet fun, featuring some eye-catching animations at the top that immediately grab the user's attention. I made sure these elements enhance the experience without distracting from the content, allowing visitors to easily find what they’re looking for.
02
interior pages
The layout for each interior page is simple and straightforward, making it easy for users to quickly find the information they need. This design approach prioritizes clarity and accessibility, enhancing the overall user experience.
03
details
I aimed to make the website a bit more fun and distinctive than typical layouts by placing the navigation at the bottom of the site. Since it’s a music label, I wanted to ensure the design was engaging and enjoyable for users.
